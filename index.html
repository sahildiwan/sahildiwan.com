Welcome!

This is SahilDiwan.com, a website about my work and experience. I bought this domain and pay for hosting just to have a place to experiment with my design skills. As you can guess, my name is Sahil Diwan. I'm a student at the University of Oregon majoring in computer science with a minor in mathematics. When I'm not in front of a computer screen you'll catch me reading NewScientist or Entrepreneur Magazine. Otherwise, I like to get together with family and friends to travel and explore. I'm also a big fan of Crossfit, so you'll always find me in the gym (or eating a box of Twinkies watching reruns of Battlestar Galactica).

I'm a chocolate lab owner from Seattle, studying in Oregon, looking to get a foot in the big tech start-up world. I have been a geek my entire life and actually began college studying to become an astronautical engineer. That is when I started working with a few start-ups and learned a lot from great developers. I found this to be the perfect career for the skills I have. After gaining some experience, I'm now ready to start my journey in this technology driven world which I have become so passionate about. I'm currently the Co-Founder and CTO of Karilla.com and am also a software development engineer intern at a tech start-up in Seattle. I'm always looking for new projects to work on so contact me!
